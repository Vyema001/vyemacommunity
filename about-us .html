About us

We are Vyema community initiative is an economic empowerment venture for small and medium entreprenuers inclined to efficient use of locally avaialable resources for optimal growth and sustenance. We seek to explore better agricultural practices in terms of fabricating farm tools that makes farming attractive, easy and enjoyable with ultimate goal to increase production beyond subsistenance farming.
